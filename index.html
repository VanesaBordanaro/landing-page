Betular Patisserie

ABOUT

Betular Patisserie is owned by Chef Damian Betular, former Executive Chef of the Palacio Duhau. It is located in Villa Devoto, on the corner of Mercedes and Asunción, one block from Plaza Arenales. The patisserie is open every day from 9am to 8pm and has a French-inspired pastry style.
Betular Patisserie offers everything you would expect from the renowned chef, including his famous macarons, truffles, artisanal bakery items, and delicious cakes and mini-cakes. The store has a 15-meter refrigerated display case full of treats to take home, as well as a visible chocolate section.
In the courtyard, there are some tables where customers can enjoy the chef's creations.
Additionally, customers can also purchase flowers, as the chef has combined his two passions in the same place: gastronomy and a flower shop.
